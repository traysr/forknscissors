# Mango Mastani

### Ingredients: 
* 2 cups mango pulp
* 1 cup chilled milk
* 2-3 tablespoon sugar
* 2 scoops mango ice cream 

#### Method:
1. Add milk, mango pulp and sugar in a blender jar. Blend until smooth and creamy. 
2. Pour the milkshake in a glass. Add 2 scoopes of mango ice cream on top. 
3. Serve chilled.
