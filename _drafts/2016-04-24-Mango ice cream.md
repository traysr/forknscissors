### Ingredients:
* Condensed milk (14 oz) 
* Mango pulp (14 oz)
* Whipped topping (8 oz)

### Method:
1. Add condensed milk, whipped topping and mango pulp in a bowl. Mix everything properly and pour it in a airtight container. 
2. Keep it in a refrigerator till set.
