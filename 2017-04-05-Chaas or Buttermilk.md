# Serves:2

### Ingredients:
* 1 cup yogurt
* Salt to taste
* Â¼ teaspoon asafoetida
* 3-4 cups chilled water

### Method:
1. Add yogurt, salt, and asafoetida in a bowl. Whisk it. 
2. Add water and whisk again. I like my buttermilk to be thin. So adjust water according to your preference. 
3. Serve immediately.
